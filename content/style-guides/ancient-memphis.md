# Ancient Memphis Visual Direction

## Working Tone

Alive, inhabited, sunlit, ceremonial, and human. The experience should feel like entering a plausible ancient district rather than visiting silent ruins.

## Provisional Motifs

- Nile approach with boats and reeds
- mudbrick walls and plastered surfaces
- whitewashed district walls as a strong Memphis identity signal
- craft activity with pottery, stone, wood, copper, linen, and baskets
- temple approach with processional symmetry
- interior paint, columns, ritual objects, smoke, and filtered light

## Evidence Reminder

This style guide is provisional. Step 2 will choose the historically strongest temple or district anchor in ancient Memphis, and Step 3 will record usable sources and licenses.
